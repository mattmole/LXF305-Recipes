# Cheese on Toast

## Serving Suggestion

Lunch for one

## Ingredients

* Slices of cheese
* Sandwich Pickle
* Two slices of bread

## Method

* Toast the bread
* When toasted, butter and spread the pickle
* Add the cheese
* Cook under a grill until the cheese has melted

## Review / Other Information

* Very quick to make
* Can change the pickle for other sauces
